# Wiki Index

This is bundled example content so the site boots out of the box. Point it at your
own wiki by setting `CLAUDE_WIKI_DIR` to your wiki root (articles live under
`<root>/articles/`), then restart.

## Learning

- [[spaced-repetition]] — interval-based review schedules to combat forgetting
- [[forgetting-curve]] — exponential decay of retention over time

Cross-link articles with `[[double-bracket]]` links. They render in Obsidian as
vault links and here as page links, and they feed the backlinks panel at the
bottom of each article.
